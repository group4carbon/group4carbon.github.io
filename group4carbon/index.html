Did you know?

The recycling rates of Textile/Leather waste dropped, as the amount exported for recycling dropped due to high freight costs compared to pre-COVID-19 levels.

The non-domestic recycling rate increased by 2 per cent to 72 per cent, while the domestic recycling rate decreased by 1 per cent to 12 per cent. The decrease in the domestic recycling rate was largely due the drop in Textile/Leather waste recycled as lower quantities were exported for recycling.

Purpose
